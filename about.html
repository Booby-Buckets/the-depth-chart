The Depth Chart

The Depth Chart is an independent college basketball roster analysis site covering every major conference program. We break down rosters the way coaches and scouts do — by depth, position, individual grades, and offseason movement.

Every team page includes a starting five depth chart, full roster with player grades and stats, offseason additions with transfer origins, key losses, and NIL evaluations. The rankings page lets you sort and filter every program by roster grade, depth, recruiting class, and NIL spending tier.

This isn't a stats aggregator. Every grade and evaluation is hand-assigned based on film, context, and roster construction — not just box scores. The goal is to give fans, bettors, and basketball people a cleaner picture of where programs actually stand heading into a season.

📋
Depth Charts
Starting five grids built from the actual roster order — not projected. Backups listed by position with individual grades.
📊
Player Grades
Every player on every roster receives an individual grade from A+ to F based on their role, production, and impact.
🔄
Transfer Tracking
Every offseason addition is tracked with their origin school. Key losses are listed for every program too.
💰
NIL Evaluations
Each program receives an NIL spending grade and tier estimate — from Tier 1 ($20M+) down to Tier 9 ($0–500K).
